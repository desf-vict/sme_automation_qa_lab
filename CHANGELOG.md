### Project Focus

This project focuses on validating whether business automations are ready for SME customer delivery.

It covers:

- automation behaviour
- customer data handling
- structured output validation
- integration readiness
- AI-assisted workflow risk
- delivery acceptance
- customer handover readiness

### QA Principle

A working demo is not the same as a deliverable automation.

Delivery readiness requires evidence, validation, documentation, and clear customer handover material.
